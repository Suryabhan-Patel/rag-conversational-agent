# AutoStream - Company Information & Policies

## About AutoStream

AutoStream is an AI-powered video editing platform designed for content creators. We help creators automate their video editing workflow, saving time and enabling them to focus on content creation.

## Key Features

- **AI-Powered Editing**: Automatically cut, trim, and arrange videos using artificial intelligence
- **Multi-Platform Optimization**: Export videos optimized for YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and other platforms
- **AI Captions**: Automatically generate captions from video audio (Pro Plan)
- **Smart Transitions**: Intelligent transition placement using AI
- **Background Removal**: One-click background removal with AI
- **Color Grading**: Automatic color correction and enhancement

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- **No refunds after 7 days** of purchase
- Refund requests must be made within 7 days of subscribing
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days
- Cancellations can be made anytime, with access continuing until the end of the billing cycle

### Support Policy
- **Basic Plan**: Email support with 24-48 hour response time
- **Pro Plan**: 24/7 priority support with average response time under 1 hour
- Support team: support@autostream.com

### Data & Privacy
- All videos are encrypted in transit and at rest
- We don't share user data with third parties
- Videos are retained for 90 days after deletion before permanent removal
- GDPR compliant

### Billing
- Monthly billing on the same day each month
- No hidden fees
- Cancel anytime, no long-term contracts
- Invoice available in account settings
